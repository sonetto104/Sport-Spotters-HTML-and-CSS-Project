What We Offer

At Sport Spotters we can find a club for any sport you want to pursue, as long as there's a club for it in London. If you feel like you need some inspiration, here are some of the most popular sports with our current clients!

Badminton

Badminton is a fast-paced game of moderate intensity. It's a great way to get your cardio in, engages the whole body, requires strategic thinking and has the added bonus of requiring minimal equipment! It tends not to be too high impact on your joints too - so many reasons to give it a go!

Basketball

This high-intensity sport will keep you on your toes and force you to try and stay a step ahead of the game if you want to score. It's a great way to make friends with your fellow teammates with regular attendance.

Circuit Training

If burning calories, breaking a sweat and getting your heart pumping is the goal, circuit training is for you. It's also a great way to mix cardio exercise with bodyweight exercises.

Cricket

Social skills, teamwork, cooperation and communication are key in cricket. It's a great way to make new friends as well as keeping fit. Spectator culture is particarly livey here in London, and let's not forget how well it goes with Pimms in the summer!

Hockey

One of the fastest moving field sports, hockey requires it all - speed, stamina, strategy and communication. A fantastic game for people who love the buzz of teamwork. Being such a popular sport, there's a broad range of competition out there!

Rowing

A very high-intensity sport, rowing requires both strength and fine skill, as well as the mental endurance to push on even when your body wants to give up. Usually teams train a lot, so having frequent early morning availability is recommended - it's perfect for building a tight-knit community.

Tennis

Young or old, everybody can play tennis. Being so popular, there are many clubs in London catering for all levels, whether you're aiming for competitive athletic matches or whether you want to enjoy it at master's level.

Touch Rugby

Touch rugby is perfect for anyone who loves the excitement of the slow-build in rugby, but doesn't want to worry about concussion or broken bones in the process! Touch rugby tends to be very social in London - a Saturday morning game is often followed by a trip to the pub!

Running

Perhaps the simplest of all options available - nothing required other than some shoes on your feet! Whether you want to go for a relaxing weekend jog along the Thames path or whether you want to train for a marathon, there's a running group in London to cater for everyone's needs.
